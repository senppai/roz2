A Few Examples of How RO&H SERVICES Can Help

With our versatile list of services, there are countless ways that RO&H SERVICES can help your company. But, let’s talk about a few specific examples to better describe our role in your workflow.
Making digital twins. Let’s say you have 20 fire extinguishers in your factory that need to be regularly inspected to meet industry requirements. Our team can create a digital twin of your factory, specifically identifying all 20 fire extinguishers. By using our updated floor plan, your maintenance team can save tons of time.
‍Contingency planning. What is your company supposed to do if a dam bursts, a flood occurs, or a nuclear reactor fails? Through documentation and planning, our team can help you prepare for the worst-case scenario. In addition, we can help develop systems that notify nearby residents if there’s a disaster on your site, like a chemical spill or explosion.
‍As-builts for emergency escapes. Most companies today need to legally have an escape plan that’s published for employees within their buildings. If there’s an attack or fire within your building, your employees need to know where to go and what to do. RO&H SERVICES can create building as-builts with emergency egresses laid out to remove any confusion when it comes to emergency escapes.
‍Process drawings and schematics. A lot of companies like to have schematics and drawings of whatever chemicals, liquids, and gases are used within their process equipment. However, other companies need to report how they handle carcinogens or dangerous materials within their facility. Our engineers can create P&IDs, wiring diagrams, and full process flow diagrams to accurately report how and where dangerous materials are used within your warehouse.
